How notifications work

When a customer places an order on your website, you'll instantly receive a WhatsApp message on your phone with the order details — customer name, items, total amount, and their contact number.


To fully activate WhatsApp notifications, you need to connect a free service called Twilio or CallMeBot. Once your site is live on GitHub, I will walk you through the 5-minute setup — no coding needed.


Telegram works the same way through a free Telegram Bot — also a 5-minute setup after your site is live.
