How to fully activate WhatsApp notifications

When a customer places an order, the website opens WhatsApp directly for them to confirm — so you receive the order details in your WhatsApp inbox automatically, with no setup needed.

For automatic background alerts (when you're not watching the admin panel), you'll need to connect a free service called CallMeBot for WhatsApp. This is a 5-minute setup I'll walk you through once your site is live.
