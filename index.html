Our journey

25 years of spice, soul, and dedication

From a small stall in the local market to kitchens across the country — this is how Kishor Spices grew.

1
1998
Where it all began
Kishor Bhai started selling hand-ground masalas from a small stall in the local market. Word spread fast — the quality was different.
2
2005
First factory, first leap
Opened our first small processing unit. Started supplying to local grocery chains and restaurants across the city.
3
2012
Going national
Kishor Spices reached 8 states. Our turmeric won a national quality award — a proud moment for the whole family.
4
2020
Going digital
Launched online — connecting directly with customers across India and abroad. 50,000 orders in the first year alone.
5
2024
25 years strong
Exporting to 12 countries. Still family-run. Still using the same sourcing philosophy Kishor Bhai started with on day one.
