Our Story

From Ghorawal
to 50,000 kitchens

घर से शुरू हुई यात्रा, आज लाखों रसोइयों तक।

It started in 1998 with a small stall at the local market in Ghorawal, Sonbhadra. The masalas were made at home, ground on stone, packed by hand. People noticed the difference immediately — the colour, the aroma, the flavour that no brand could replicate.

Twenty-five years later, Kishor Spices has grown to serve over 50,000 kitchens across India and beyond. But one thing has never changed — every spice we sell is something we'd use in our own kitchen.
